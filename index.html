What even is a healthcare CRM?

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a technology that enables Mental Health Clinics to manage their interactions with customers and potential customers. The primary goal of CRM is to improve relationships with customers to foster growth.

In your work, you have two types of customers:

Those who are being referred to you...
...your potential clients.
Those who make referrals to you...
...your business contacts.

Your CRM should handle both.

So why is this important to your business?

Faster time to service

Getting client's through the door is your jam.

Revenue increase

Keeping your openings filled means keeping your lights on.

Data-driven decision-making

Operating from your gut only gets you so far.

Transparency & organization

Your front-end operations set the tone for service.
