We built Supportable out of necessity. After 30+ years of experience providing services to individuals in the Mental Health, Behavioral Health, Human Services, and Substance Use industry, we kept searching for but never found a solution to help us truly organize our intake operations.

Supportable is an all-in-one intake software designed with a simple design for intuitive use. Our software hosts many features, like single-point insurance verification, pre-screening questionnaires, and custom checklists. You can manage client progress through our all-encompassing, customizable process to reduce your workload.
